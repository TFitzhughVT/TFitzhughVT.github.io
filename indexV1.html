STREETDREAMS HERITAGE PARK PHOTOWALK

Take a behind the scenes look of the Heritage Park Photowalk hosted by Streetdreams, which is the Virginia Tech Photography Club. Streetdreams hosts many events throughout the semester such as photowalks in which members come together to walk around, talk, and take pictures at a designated location. If you have any interest in photography and go to Virginia Tech you should join Streetdreams and meet more people who share a passion for photography.

PHOTOGRAPHY

My photography site hosts previous automotive pictures I have taken. From car meets, to drifting events, and personal shoots it has it all. Feel free to stop by and take a look. Although this site only has automotive photos, my Instagram has all types of photos including portaits, street photography, and more.

SOCIAL MEDIA STANDARDS FOR GUYS & GIRLS

This podcast is made by Kangaroos group in ENGL 3844. We talk about the possible different social media standards for guys and girls. This is a joint effort of several students at Virginia Tech who all have different backgrounds and thoughts on the topic.

3 STEPS TO USING PRESETS IN 2022

There’s no denying the popularity of presets in 2022 and there’s a good reason for that. I talk about 3 steps to using Presets in 2020 in this blog post on medium. Photographers of all experience should understand presets, how they work, and when to use them. Be sure to check the blog post out to learn more.
